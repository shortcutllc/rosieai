1 Compact Header
300px header → 56px. Clock, weather, and age badge compressed into a single row. Greeting moves below as a left-aligned hero title (Airbnb pattern).
2 Tab Icons
Text-only tabs gain small icons above labels. Inspired by Airbnb's 3D icon tabs. Adds visual personality without adding clutter.
3 Horizontal Stat Carousel
Summary cards change from a cramped 3-column grid to a horizontally-scrolling carousel with scroll-snap. Each card gets more breathing room. A 4th "Today totals" card appears.
4 Section Title + Arrow Pattern
Every content section follows Airbnb's pattern: bold 20px title on the left, "See all →" link on the right. Creates consistent visual rhythm between sections.
5 Integrated Insight Card
A developmental insight from "This Week" appears inline on the main screen — no tab switch needed. Gradient background makes it visually distinct from timeline events.
6 Lighter Timeline Events
Event cards use lighter typography (15px/600 vs 18px/700), time pushed to the right edge (Airbnb's secondary data placement), and tighter padding. More events visible per screen.
7 Unified Quick Log Pill
Proposed A: Three separate floating buttons merge into a single pill with dividers. Less visual noise, same functionality.
8 Consistent Section Spacing
All sections maintain equal 24px vertical gaps (Airbnb's ~304px section rhythm scaled to our content density). Creates a predictable scanning pattern.
9 No Clock
Proposed B: Clock removed entirely — phones already show time in the status bar. Frees up the entire top section for what matters: greeting + baby info.
10 Cards ARE the Actions
Proposed B: No floating bottom bar. Each stat card has a "+ Feed" / "+ Sleep" / "+ Diaper" button embedded in it. Like Airbnb — you tap a listing card to book, not a separate button. Status and action unified in one place.
11 No Bottom Bar = More Timeline
Proposed B: Removing the 80px floating bottom bar means 2 more timeline events visible at once. The action cards at the top handle logging. Scroll = pure content.
12 Minimal Header (48px)
Proposed B: Header compressed further to just weather pill + avatar. Greeting is the page hero. Total overhead before content: ~140px vs current ~300px.
